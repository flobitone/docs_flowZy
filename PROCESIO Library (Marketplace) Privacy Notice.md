# PROCESIO Library (Marketplace) Privacy Notice

## Privacy Notice

**Version:** 1.0\
**Effective Date:** 2026-01-19\
**Last Updated:** 2026-01-19

This Privacy Notice explains how PROCESIO processes personal data in
connection with the PROCESIO Library, also referred to as the
"Marketplace", in accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation
(EU) 2016/679 ("GDPR").

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 1. Data Controller

PROCESIO is the data controller for personal data processed in
connection with the Marketplace.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 2. Personal Data We Process

We may process the following categories of personal data:

-   account information (name, email address, user ID)\
-   Marketplace activity (uploads, downloads, imports, verification
    status)\
-   logs related to Marketplace usage\
-   records of acceptance of Terms, Disclaimer, and Privacy Notice

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 3. Purposes of Processing

Personal data is processed for the following purposes:

-   operating and securing the Marketplace\
-   enabling content sharing and usage\
-   verification and review of Marketplace Content\
-   abuse prevention and security monitoring\
-   legal compliance and dispute resolution

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 4. Legal Bases for Processing

Processing is based on:

-   performance of a contract (Terms of Agreement)\
-   PROCESIO's legitimate interests (platform security and integrity)\
-   compliance with legal obligations

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 5. Marketplace-Specific GDPR Clarification

PROCESIO does not access, monitor, or control the data processed by
user-created or Marketplace automations.

Users remain the data controllers for any personal data processed
through automations they create, share, or use.

PROCESIO does not determine the purposes or means of such processing and
does not act as a processor for automation-executed data.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 6. Third Parties

Personal data may be shared with:

-   infrastructure and hosting providers\
-   security and monitoring providers\
-   payment providers (if applicable in the future)

PROCESIO does not sell personal data.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 7. Data Retention

Personal data is retained only for as long as necessary to fulfill the
purposes described above or to comply with legal obligations.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 8. Your Rights Under GDPR

You have the right to:

-   access your personal data\
-   request rectification or erasure\
-   request restriction of processing or object to processing\
-   data portability\
-   lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 9. Security

PROCESIO implements appropriate technical and organizational measures to
protect personal data. However, no system can be guaranteed to be
completely secure.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 10. Changes to This Privacy Notice

This Privacy Notice may be updated from time to time. Continued use of
the Marketplace indicates awareness of the updated notice.
